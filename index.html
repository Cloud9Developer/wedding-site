How we met

We went our whole lives basically living next door—Chris growing up in Avondale; Mary growing up in Chalmette—but never actually meeting until God decided it was our time.

We first met through an online dating app just before Christmas of 2024. Once we matched, our conversations flowed naturally and constantly. Whenever one of us would go to sleep, we would pick up the very next morning as if the conversation never stopped.

After the holidays (and Chris getting over a minor/man cold :) ), we decided to go out on our first date—Thai, then Escapology. After failing to escape, but having an amazing time together, we agreed to have a second date the following week.

Obviously, the subsequent dates continued to go extremely well, and after the third date, we decided to make it official and become the amazing couple you know and adore today! :)
